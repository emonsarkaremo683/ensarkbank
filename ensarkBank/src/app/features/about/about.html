About Ensark Bank

Building trust through innovation and service excellence since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ensark Bank has grown from a single branch in Dhaka to one of the most trusted financial institutions in Bangladesh, with over 50 branches serving more than 100,000 customers nationwide.

Our mission is to provide secure, accessible, and innovative banking solutions that empower individuals and businesses to achieve their financial goals. We combine traditional banking values with cutting-edge technology to deliver an exceptional banking experience.

At Ensark Bank, we believe in building long-term relationships with our customers based on trust, transparency, and personalized service.
